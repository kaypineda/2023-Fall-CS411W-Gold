Problem Description

Societal Problem

People have many tasks and comparatively little time. People have difficulty organizing and prioritizing tasks. A person may create a schedule only to realize the schedule does not work for that person, resulting in that person starting over.


Problem Characteristics

  • People have difficulties managing tasks.
  • They find it is difficult to prioritize and schedule tasks.
    • In a 2022 study of 500 employees across several industries, less than 1 in 5 people (18%) have a proper time management system.
    • 82% of people just use a list or their email inbox as a time management tool.
  • For those who do plan, they find their plans to be too rigid and fail to follow through.
    • Only 1 in 5 (20%) people carry out a monthly time audit to review how they are spending their time.
